About Our Masjid

Learn about our history, mission, and community

Our Story

Baytul Aman Jame Masjid was established in 2016 with a vision to create a spiritual sanctuary for Muslims in our community. What started as a small prayer space has grown into a vibrant center of worship, learning, and community service.

Our masjid serves as more than just a place of prayer. It is a beacon of hope, a center for Islamic education, and a gathering place where Muslims from diverse backgrounds come together in unity and brotherhood.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and programs to better serve our growing community, offering Quran classes, Islamic studies, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives.

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming space for worship, foster Islamic knowledge, strengthen community bonds, and promote the values of peace, compassion, and service to humanity.

Our Vision

To be a leading Islamic center that inspires spiritual growth, educates future generations, and contributes positively to the broader community while maintaining authentic Islamic teachings.

Our Journey

2016 - Foundation

Baytul Aman Jame Masjid was officially established with the first congregational prayer held.

2018 - Expansion

Prayer hall expanded to accommodate growing congregation and added ablution facilities.

2020 - Education Center

Launched dedicated Islamic education programs for children and adults.

2023 - Community Programs

Expanded community outreach with food distribution, counseling, and youth activities.

2025 - Future Plans

Ongoing expansion project to build a modern multi-purpose community center.
